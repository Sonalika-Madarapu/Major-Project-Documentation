Analyzing Diamond Features: ANN-Based Price Estimation

In my project, "Diamond Price Predictor," I employ Artificial Neural Networks (ANNs) to predict diamond prices with precision. By analyzing characteristics like shape, size, color, and clarity, the ANN model uncovers hidden patterns. This project aids gemstone enthusiasts and investors in making informed decisions within the diamond market. My goal is to achieve high accuracy in price prediction, bridging technology and gemology for valuable insights into precious stone valuation.
